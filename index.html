Overview

CARL-D comprises of a large-scale stereo vision-based driving videos captured from more than 100 cities of Pakistan. As a benchmark selection, 15,000 suitable images are labelled for 2D object detection and recognition. Whereas semantic segmentation benchmark contains 2500 images have pixel-level high quality fine annotations along with 5000 coarse-annotated images which could help in enabling the deep neural networks to leverage the weakly labeled data.

Object Detection & Recognition

2D 15,000 images

Our object detection and recognition benchmark comprising of 15,000 annotations with around 50,348 labels are presented in 2x formats (i.e., Yolo and VOC PASCAL). The proposed object detection dataset contains 25 diverse classes. Moreover, one-stage and two-stage object detectors are evaluated on the proposed benchmark to demonstrate the correctness/effectiveness of our dataset.
